Frequently Asked Questions

How does the e-Stamp process work? +

Once you fill in the agreement details, our system calculates the government stamp duty based on your state. After payment, a digital e-Stamp is generated from the authorized government portal and merged with your agreement for digital signing.

Is my Aadhaar data safe? +

Yes, we use 256-bit encryption. We do not store your Aadhaar number; we only receive a masked verification token from UIDAI-authorized partners to confirm your identity.

Can I cancel a booking? +

Cancellations are subject to the specific property's policy. Most bookings offer a 24-hour window for a full refund of the token amount if the agreement hasn't been initiated.

Policies & Guidelines

Terms & Conditions

1. User Obligations

Users must provide accurate, current, and complete information during the registration process. Misrepresentation of identity or property details is grounds for immediate account termination.

2. Rental Contracts

Nepxall is a technology service provider. The actual rental contract is between the Owner and the Tenant. We are not responsible for disputes regarding property maintenance or house rules unless they violate local laws.

3. Payment Gateway

Payments are processed via secure third-party gateways (Cashfree/Razorpay). Nepxall is not liable for banking delays or failed transactions caused by user bank servers.

4. Payment & Settlement

All payments are processed through RBI-authorized payment gateways. NEPXALL TECHNOLOGIES does not store card or banking information.
